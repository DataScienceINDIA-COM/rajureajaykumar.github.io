Datorama Reporting Ecosystem

Goal: To create a centralized, automated, and scalable reporting platform for over 800 marketing campaigns, enabling real-time insights and reducing manual reporting effort.

Technologies: Datorama, Google Ads, Meta, The Trade Desk, Double Verify, IAS, Pinterest, Bing Ads, Walmart

Architected real-time reporting for 800+ campaigns, integrating multiple platforms and reducing report turnaround time by 70%. Standardized data with Harmonization Center and designed dynamic dashboards for stakeholders.
