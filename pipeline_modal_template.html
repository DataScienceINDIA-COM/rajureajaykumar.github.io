Optimized Data Pipeline for Marketing Campaigns

Goal: To establish a robust and efficient data pipeline that automates the flow of data from various marketing sources, enabling timely analysis and reporting for campaign optimization.

Technologies: ETL Tools (e.g., Dataiku, Alteryx, or custom scripts), Data Warehouse (e.g., Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery), BI Tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker, Power BI), Data Connectors.

This diagram illustrates a comprehensive data pipeline designed to support the data needs of a marketing campaign, from raw data ingestion to actionable insights.

    graph TD
        A[Marketing Platforms (Ads, Social, CRM, etc.)] --> B(Data Extraction via APIs/Connectors);
        B --> C(Data Ingestion into Staging Area);
        C --> D(Data Cleaning & Validation);
        D --> E(Data Transformation & Modeling);
        E --> F[Data Warehouse/Lake];
        F --> G(Data Aggregation & Mart Creation);
        G --> H[Business Intelligence Tools];
        H --> I[Interactive Marketing Dashboards];
        I --> A;
        I --> J[Analysis & Reporting];
        J --> K[Campaign Optimization Decisions];
